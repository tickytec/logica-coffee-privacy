Logica Coffee

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026 — iCloud sync added

Logica Coffee does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your data stays under your control — on your device and, if you choose, in your own iCloud account.

Overview

Logica Coffee is a personal coffee brewing companion. Your brew journal, bean inventory, recipes, and settings are stored on your device and — if you have iCloud enabled — synced across your devices via your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any servers, and no data is ever sent to us. No account is required.

Data stored on your device

The following information is saved on your device and, if iCloud is enabled, in your personal iCloud account:

  • Brew journal entries (method, dose, ratio, tasting notes, star rating)
  • Coffee bean inventory (name, origin, roast level, flavour notes, remaining weight)
  • Custom recipes and brew timelines you create
  • App preferences and settings (grinder, temperature, filter type)

This data is not accessible to us or any third party. You can delete it at any time from within the app or by uninstalling the app. You can disable iCloud sync for Logica Coffee at any time in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud on your iPhone.

iCloud sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Logica Coffee uses Apple's iCloud Documents service to sync your journal, bean inventory, and recipes across your iPhone and any other devices signed into the same Apple ID. This sync happens through your personal iCloud account — the data is stored by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy and is not accessible to us in any way.

iCloud sync is entirely optional and controlled by your device settings. If iCloud is unavailable or disabled, the app stores your data locally on-device as normal.

Apple Watch sync

If you use the Logica Coffee Apple Watch app, brew session data is shared between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework. This communication happens directly between your two devices over your local network or Bluetooth — it does not pass through any external server.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or track you across apps
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Third-party services

Logica Coffee does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or crash reporting services. The only external frameworks used are Apple's own system frameworks (WatchConnectivity, WidgetKit, ActivityKit, and iCloud Documents), all of which operate under Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's privacy

Logica Coffee does not knowingly collect any data from users of any age. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way (for example, if a future version of the app introduces network features), the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that, and the new policy will be available before the update reaches the App Store.
