Frequently Asked Questions

Is Logica Coffee free?

Yes, completely. No subscription, no paywall, no ads. Every feature is fully unlocked from the moment you install. Optional tips are available inside if you'd like to support development — entirely your choice.

Where is my data stored?

All your brew journal entries, bean inventory, and custom recipes are stored on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, they sync automatically across your devices via your personal iCloud account. Nothing is sent to any external server.

How do I back up or transfer my data to a new iPhone?

If iCloud sync is enabled, your data transfers automatically when you sign in on a new device. Alternatively, restoring from an iCloud or iTunes backup will include all Logica Coffee data.

The Apple Watch app isn't syncing with my iPhone. What do I do?

Make sure both devices are on the same Apple ID and that Bluetooth is enabled. Open the app on your iPhone first, then launch it on your Watch. If the problem persists, force-quitting and reopening both apps usually resolves it.

How do I create a custom recipe?

In the Brew Builder, select a method, then tap the recipe selector and choose "Create Recipe". You can add as many stages as you like, setting pour amount, timing, and water type for each one.

What brew methods does the app support?

Hario V60, AeroPress, Chemex, French Press, Hario Switch, Clever Dripper, Moka Pot, Syphon, Cold Brew, Turkish Coffee, Vietnamese Phin, Ceado Hoop, Espresso, Ristretto, Lungo, Americano, Cappuccino, Flat White, Latte, Cortado, and Macchiato.

How do I delete all my data?

You can delete individual journal entries and beans from within the app. To remove all data, uninstall the app. If iCloud sync is enabled, you can also remove Logica Coffee data from Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at khatskevich.mv@gmail.com with a description of what happened, your iPhone model, and your iOS version. Screenshots are always helpful.
